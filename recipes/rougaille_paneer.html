Rougaille Paneer

Ingredients

  • 1 lb tomato (~ 8 medium sized tomatoes)
  • ~ 12 paneer cubes
  • 1 onion
  • 4 curry leaves
  • Thyme leaves (~ 5-6 sprigs)
  • Spring onions
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Ghee

Procedure

  1. Remove curry leaves and thyme leaves and wash.
  2. Add tomatoes to boiling water.
  3. Remove after 1 minute and immediately immerse in cold water.
  4. Peel tomatoes and crush by hand.
  5. Wash, peel and cut onions to desired size.
  6. Wash and finely cut coriander leaves and spring onions.
  7. Add ghee to cooking pot and heat.
  8. Add paneer to ghee and fry till pale brown colour is seen.
  9. Remove paneer from cooking pot.
  10. Add curry and thyme leaves to ghee and fry.
  11. Add cut onions to ghee and cook gently.
  12. Add tomatoes to cooking pot.
  13. Add salt and cover.
  14. Allow to cook till tomato sauce thickens.
  15. Add fried paneer to tomato sauce and turn off flame.
  16. Garnish with cut coriander leaves and spring onions.
  17. Serve hot.
  18. Enjoy your Rougaille Paneer!
